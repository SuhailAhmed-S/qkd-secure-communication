The Quantum Advantage

What is Quantum Key Distribution?

QKD leverages quantum mechanics to distribute encryption keys with proven information-theoretic security. Unlike classical cryptography, which relies on computational hardness, QKD is unbreakable by principle—any eavesdropping attempt introduces detectable errors.

Our BB84 implementation demonstrates this revolutionary approach with real-time eavesdropping detection and privacy amplification.

Eavesdropping Detection

The quantum channel betrays any unauthorized access. When Eve intercepts qubits, she introduces measurement errors that reveal her presence through elevated QBER (Quantum Bit Error Rate).

Future-Proof

Quantum-resistant from day one. Would remain secure even against quantum computers.

⚛️ BB84 Protocol Overview

The BB84 protocol enables two parties (Alice and Bob) to establish a shared secret key through quantum communication:

1
Preparation
Alice generates random bits and random bases
2
Encoding
Encode bits into quantum states using bases
3
Transmission
Send qubits through quantum channel
4
Measurement
Bob measures in random bases
5
Sifting
Compare bases, keep matching bits
6
Verification
Calculate QBER, detect eavesdropping

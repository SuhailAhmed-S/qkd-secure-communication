BB84 Protocol Overview

1
Qubit Preparation
Alice generates random bits and encodes them into quantum states using random bases (+ or ×).
2
Quantum Transmission
Qubits are transmitted through the quantum channel. Eve may optionally intercept and measure.
3
Random Measurement
Bob randomly selects measurement bases and measures each qubit, recording results.
4
Basis Reconciliation
Alice and Bob publicly compare bases (not bits) and keep only matching basis measurements.
5
QBER Calculation
Calculate quantum bit error rate to detect eavesdropping. Threshold: < 11% for secure channel.
6
Key Derivation
Use sifted key with SHA-256 to derive cryptographic key for message encryption.
